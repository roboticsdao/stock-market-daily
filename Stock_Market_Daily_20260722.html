中国人民银行 — 中国央行连续第14个月维持贷款市场报价利率不变

The People's Bank of China (PBoC) announced on July 20, 2026, that it would maintain its one-year and five-year Loan Prime Rates (LPRs) at 3% and 3.5% respectively, marking the 14th consecutive month of unchanged rates. This decision, reported on July 21, aligns with market expectations and the central bank's "moderately loose" policy stance. The stability in LPRs suggests the PBoC is balancing economic support with financial stability, avoiding aggressive easing despite some calls for further stimulus.

总结：中国人民银行于2026年7月20日宣布，将一年期和五年期贷款市场报价利率（LPR）分别维持在3%和3.5%不变，这已是连续第14个月保持不变。此举符合市场预期和央行“适度宽松”的政策立场，表明中国央行在平衡经济支持与金融稳定。
