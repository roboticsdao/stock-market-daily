Apple (AAPL) — 苹果股价因产品涨价而大幅下跌

Apple's stock plunged over 6% on Thursday, June 25th, wiping out approximately $275 billion from its market value, after the company announced significant price hikes for its MacBooks and iPads. CEO Tim Cook cited surging memory and storage chip costs, driven by AI demand, as "unavoidable" reasons for the increases. This move raised concerns among investors about potential demand destruction and the impact on Apple's profit margins, leading to its largest single-day drop since April 2025.

总结：苹果公司股价周四（6月25日）下跌超过6%，市值蒸发约2750亿美元，此前公司宣布上调MacBook和iPad价格以应对存储芯片成本飙升。投资者担忧此举可能导致需求下降并影响利润率，这是苹果自2025年4月以来的最大单日跌幅。

Microsoft (MSFT) — 微软股价跌至52周新低，面临多重压力

Microsoft's stock fell over 3% on Thursday, June 25th, hitting a new 52-week low and marking its worst June performance since 2000. The decline is attributed to a combination of factors, including heavy capital expenditure on AI initiatives, which is impacting free cash flow, and preliminary EU antitrust findings designating Azure as a "gatekeeper" under the Digital Markets Act. Analysts are re-evaluating the stock from a cash-flow compounder to a heavy-infrastructure story, contributing to a broader rotation out of "Magnificent Seven" tech stocks.

总结：微软股价周四（6月25日）下跌超过3%，创下52周新低，并面临2000年以来最差的6月表现。股价下跌主要受AI巨额资本支出、自由现金流下降以及欧盟反垄断调查等多重因素影响，市场正重新评估其投资前景。

Micron Technology (MU) — 美光科技强劲财报提振芯片股

总结：美光科技股价周四（6月25日）飙升近16%，此前公司公布了超出预期的财报和乐观展望。这一利好消息重振了市场对人工智能相关芯片股的信心，提振了包括闪迪和高通在内的其他芯片制造商，显示AI对存储芯片的需求依然强劲。

Federal Reserve — 美国通胀超4%，美联储加息预期升温

US inflation, as measured by the Federal Reserve's preferred metric (Core PCE), increased above 4.0% in May for the first time in three years, driven by higher energy prices. This persistent inflation, coupled with a resilient labor market, has shifted market expectations, with traders now anticipating the Fed to potentially lift interest rates by at least 25 basis points before year-end, rather than initiating rate cuts. The "hawkish pause" signals a longer-than-expected period of restrictive monetary policy.

总结：美国5月份通胀率（美联储首选指标）升至4%以上，为三年来首次，主要受能源价格上涨推动。持续的通胀和强劲的劳动力市场使市场对美联储加息的预期增强，而非此前预期的降息，预示限制性货币政策可能持续更久。
