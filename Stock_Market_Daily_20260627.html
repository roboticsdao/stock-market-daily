US Market Indices — 科技股抛售拖累美股主要指数下跌

Major US stock indices experienced declines for the week ending June 27, 2026, primarily driven by a significant sell-off in AI-linked technology and semiconductor stocks. The S&P 500 fell 0.05% on Friday, concluding its second losing week in 13, while the Nasdaq Composite dropped 0.24% for the day and approximately 4% for the week, marking its worst weekly performance in over a year. The Dow Jones Industrial Average also slipped 0.09% on Friday but managed a slight weekly gain due to a rotation into defensive sectors like healthcare. Concerns over AI spending returns and persistent inflation contributed to the cautious market sentiment.

总结：截至2026年6月27日，受人工智能相关科技股和半导体股票大幅抛售影响，美国主要股指本周普遍下跌。标普500指数周五下跌0.05%，录得近13周以来的第二个周跌幅；纳斯达克综合指数周五下跌0.24%，本周累计下跌约4%，创一年多来最差周表现。道琼斯工业平均指数周五小幅下跌0.09%，但由于资金转向防御性板块，本周略有上涨。市场对AI投资回报和持续通胀的担忧加剧了谨慎情绪。

Apple (AAPL) — 苹果因芯片成本上涨上调产品价格，股价波动后反弹

Apple Inc. experienced significant stock volatility this week, initially seeing a 6.12% drop on June 25th, its worst single-day performance since April 2025, after announcing mid-cycle price increases for Macs, iPads, and other products globally. This move was attributed to rising memory and storage chip costs, driven by insatiable demand from AI infrastructure. However, Apple's stock rebounded 3.1% on Friday, June 26th, recovering some of its losses, as investors weighed the company's pricing power against the cost pressures. Analysts noted Apple's ability to pass costs to its premium customer base, suggesting the sell-off might present a buying opportunity.

总结：苹果公司本周股价波动剧烈，因内存和存储芯片成本上涨，宣布全球范围内上调Mac、iPad等产品价格后，其股价在6月25日下跌6.12%，创下一年多来最大单日跌幅。然而，苹果股价在6月26日反弹3.1%，收复部分失地。分析师认为，尽管面临成本压力，但苹果凭借其强大的定价能力和忠实用户群，此次回调可能为投资者提供了买入机会。

NVIDIA (NVDA) / Chip Stocks — AI芯片股遭遇抛售，英伟达市值跌破5万亿美元

The AI-linked chip sector experienced a sharp sell-off this week, with the PHLX chip index dropping 5.3% on Friday and 7.9% for the week, its worst weekly performance since early April. Federal Reserve — 美联储维持利率不变，点阵图预示鹰派转向

The Federal Reserve maintained the federal funds rate unchanged at 3.50%-3.75% in its June 2026 meeting, aligning with market expectations. However, the accompanying "dot plot" revealed a significant hawkish shift, with nine out of eighteen officials now projecting at least one rate hike by the end of 2026, a notable change from earlier anticipations of rate cuts. Minneapolis Federal Reserve President Neel Kashkari explicitly stated his expectation for a rate hike this year, citing persistent inflation concerns after May's US inflation data rose above 4%. This signals a more aggressive stance on monetary policy to combat inflationary pressures.

总结：美联储在2026年6月的会议上维持联邦基金利率在3.50%-3.75%不变，符合市场预期。然而，随后的“点阵图”显示出明显的鹰派转向，18位官员中有9位预计2026年底前至少加息一次，与此前降息预期形成鲜明对比。明尼阿波利斯联储主席尼尔·卡什卡利明确表示，鉴于5月份美国通胀率升至4%以上，他预计今年将加息，这预示着更积极的货币政策立场以应对通胀压力。
