June CPI & Federal Reserve — 6月CPI低于预期，美联储加息压力减小

The June Consumer Price Index (CPI) rose 3.5% annually, falling below the 3.8% forecast and marking a 0.4% month-over-month decline, the largest since 2020, largely due to falling energy prices. This softer inflation reading has significantly reduced the probability of a Federal Reserve rate hike in July, providing crucial breathing room for newly appointed Fed Chair Kevin Warsh, who testified to Congress about combating inflation without signaling immediate policy changes.

总结：6月份美国消费者物价指数（CPI）年率上涨3.5%，低于预期的3.8%，环比下降0.4%，为2020年以来最大月度跌幅，主要受能源价格下跌影响。这一低于预期的通胀数据显著降低了美联储7月加息的可能性，为新任主席凯文·沃什提供了政策空间，尽管他在国会证词中未明确下一步行动。

IBM — IBM第二季度业绩预警导致股价暴跌

IBM's shares plummeted by approximately 22-25% after the company issued a preliminary second-quarter earnings warning, missing Wall Street expectations. CEO Arvind Krishna attributed the shortfall to a surprise shift in client capital expenditure towards server, storage, and memory purchases, impacting its software and infrastructure businesses. This significant decline made IBM the heaviest drag on the Dow Jones Industrial Average, despite broader market gains.

总结：IBM股价在发布第二季度初步财报预警后暴跌约22-25%，未能达到华尔街预期。首席执行官Arvind Krishna将业绩下滑归咎于客户资本支出意外转向服务器、存储和内存采购，影响了其软件和基础设施业务。此次大幅下跌使IBM成为道琼斯工业平均指数的最大拖累。
