US Market — 股市受AI和科技股提振走高，但风险信号显现

The major U.S. stock indexes closed higher, largely driven by continued enthusiasm for artificial intelligence and technology shares. The Dow Jones Industrial Average gained 0.29% to 52,637.01, the S&P 500 advanced 0.42% to 7,575.39, and the Nasdaq 100 rose 0.33% to 29,825.11. Year-to-date, the Dow is up 9.5%, Nasdaq 13.1%, and S&P 500 10.7%, with the rally increasingly dependent on AI. However, some analysts note increasing risk flags, including surging speculative trading in leveraged ETFs and high investor allocations to stocks, suggesting potential volatility ahead.

总结：美国主要股指在人工智能和科技股的持续热情推动下收高。道琼斯工业平均指数上涨0.29%，标普500指数上涨0.42%，纳斯达克100指数上涨0.33%。尽管市场表现强劲，但分析师指出，杠杆ETF投机交易激增和投资者股票配置处于高位，预示着未来可能出现波动。

SK Hynix — SK海力士在纳斯达克首次亮相表现强劲

South Korean memory-chip giant SK Hynix made a strong debut on the Nasdaq, with its American Depositary Receipts (ADRs) jumping 13% above their offering price to close at $168.01 after opening at $170. The company raised $26.5 billion in what was reported as the largest U.S. share sale by a foreign company. SK Hynix's CEO highlighted that the global memory chip supply shortage could extend beyond 2030, reinforcing expectations for robust pricing and demand within the industry, particularly for high-bandwidth memory (HBM) crucial for AI systems.

总结：韩国存储芯片巨头SK海力士在纳斯达克首次亮相表现强劲，其美国存托凭证（ADRs）较发行价上涨13%，收于168.01美元。此次发行融资265亿美元，是外国公司在美国进行的最大规模股票发行。公司CEO表示，全球内存芯片短缺可能持续到2030年以后，预示着AI所需的高带宽内存需求和价格将保持强劲。

Apple — 苹果公司起诉OpenAI涉嫌窃取商业机密

Apple Inc. filed a lawsuit against OpenAI, accusing the artificial intelligence company of unlawfully stealing trade secrets to develop competitive hardware. This legal action signifies a major deterioration in the relationship between the two tech giants, who had previously partnered in 2024 to integrate ChatGPT into Apple's Siri. Apple alleges that former employees who joined OpenAI took confidential technical information, and OpenAI is now using this intellectual property to build its own hardware, a claim OpenAI has publicly denied.

总结：苹果公司对OpenAI提起诉讼，指控这家人工智能公司非法窃取商业机密以开发竞争性硬件。此举标志着两家科技巨头关系显著恶化，此前它们曾于2024年合作将ChatGPT整合到苹果Siri中。苹果声称OpenAI利用前员工窃取的机密技术开发自有硬件，但OpenAI否认了这些指控。

Federal Reserve — 美联储7月加息预期升温

Speculation is intensifying regarding a potential interest rate hike by the Federal Reserve at its upcoming July 28-29 meeting. Fed Governor Christopher Waller's recent remarks suggest the central bank's focus has "completely flipped" from labor market concerns to inflation containment. As of June 2026, the Federal Reserve maintained its benchmark federal funds rate at a target range of 3.50% to 3.75%. 总结：市场对美联储在即将到来的7月28-29日会议上可能加息的猜测日益升温。美联储理事克里斯托弗·沃勒最近的言论表明，央行的重心已“完全转向”抑制通胀而非劳动力市场担忧。截至2026年6月，美联储将联邦基金利率目标区间维持在3.50%至3.75%。目前市场预计7月加息25个基点的可能性约为25%，反映出美联储更趋鹰派的立场。
